About Us

Join us for India's first-ever virtually hosted and student-led hackathon. Compete individually or in a pair to create imaginative software which solves real-world problems.

  • Experienced mentors
  • Workshops to facilitate project ideation
  • Virtual support throughout the event

We invite you to let creativity and innovation blossom as you create software, not just to make a change, but also for fun. We want you to invent stuff, and we can't wait to see how you do it.

Tracks

Choose any of the tracks below as a foundation for your project.

Assistive tech

Use your tech-skills to assist a wide range of individuals, from the elderly to those with disabilities. Develop tools make their lives easier!

FinTech

Analyze the stock market, create a cryptocurrency, or improve the banking experience. The future of finance is in your hands!

Health

Fitness, sleep, and exercise are among the most important parts of our lives. Make it easier for people to track or improve some aspect of their personal health.

Science

Build tools to help further scientific discovery. You could train a network classifier to identify celestial objects, or a model to diagnose diseases. To infinity and beyond!


Entertainment

Build a new game or puzzle. There's literally no limits to what you can do with entertainment.

Social good

Build tools which help your fellow humans give back to their communities or environment. Encourage people to make a difference.

Education

Technology has changed the way we learn. Build tools to help enrich the learning experience for students all over the globe.

Developer tools

Build tools to help your fellow developers write better code. Make products more secure, create an extension for your IDE, or build an AI programming assistant.
